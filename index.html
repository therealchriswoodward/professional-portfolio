Chris Woodward is an author, business strategist, programmer, financial and data analyst, and family man.
First and foremost in his life is his family; then he is a self-motivated and highly driven leader. Chris's
multifaceted skill set along with his unique personality and diverse background bring a rare and fresh perspective
on navigating a business in different seasons.

"I don't believe in cookie cutter leadership. Spending time staring at a cell phone trying to learn
how teenage girls and cute party boys would run the company is not a winning strategy. Business leadership
today requires technological skills, complex problem-solving skills, people skills, and just as important:
a personality and the ability to get employees to enjoy their time at work and actually want
to be there. An MBA and a smartphone just won't cut it in today's market.
It's time to make a paradigm shift."

- Chris Woodward
